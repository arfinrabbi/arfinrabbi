Valicha Restaurant Menu

This menu design for Valicha captures the vibrant spirit of Peruvian cuisine through bold orange and red tones that reflect traditional flavors and charcoal cooking methods. The playful typography and hand-drawn elements create an approachable, family-friendly atmosphere while maintaining clear information hierarchy for easy navigation.


The layout strategically highlights signature items like Charcoal Chicken and the Family Feast, with the "Classic Chips" section serving as a central focal point. Each menu section is thoughtfully organized to guide customers through the authentic Peruvian offerings, from Inca Wings to traditional Papa Rellena.


The design successfully balances appetite appeal with functional readability, creating an effective branding tool that communicates Valicha's identity as an authentic Peruvian eatery celebrating both traditional recipes and modern presentation.

Praemium Corporate Business Card

This business card for Praemium features a sophisticated two-tone design using light gray and deep teal, with an interlocking geometric element that creates visual interest while maintaining corporate professionalism. The clean, minimalist aesthetic effectively communicates the company's executive-level positioning and modern approach to business.


The layout strategically separates branding from contact information, with the company logo and tagline "gruppe. einfach. mehr." on the left panel, while the right side showcases digital presence and reinforced messaging. The geometric interlocking shape serves as both a design element and subtle metaphor for business connectivity.


The design successfully balances modern sophistication with functional clarity, presenting Klaus Tenbrock's Managing Partner credentials in a hierarchy that reflects Praemium's professional corporate identity and commitment to streamlined business communication.

GDN Investment Group Business Card

This business card design for GDN Investment Group features a sophisticated logo combining a golden tree with green stems and a yellow arc, symbolizing growth, prosperity, and financial stability. The elegant color palette of sage green, gold, and soft gray creates a premium feel appropriate for an investment firm, while the marble texture background adds subtle luxury and professionalism.


The design maintains excellent visual hierarchy with the company logo prominently displayed and Dean Godden's credentials as Director clearly presented. The contact information is organized with intuitive golden icons for phone, email, and other communication methods, ensuring easy readability and professional presentation across both sides of the card.


The overall aesthetic successfully communicates trust, growth, and financial expertise through thoughtful use of natural imagery and premium materials. The tree motif reinforces the investment theme of cultivating wealth over time, making it an effective branding tool that aligns perfectly with GDN Investment Group's corporate identity and market positioning.

Peru Chicken Menu

This menu design features a rich purple background with elegant gold framing, creating a premium yet approachable aesthetic for Peru Chicken. The playful chicken mascot establishes friendly brand personality while the sophisticated color palette elevates the dining experience, successfully balancing fun elements with professional presentation.


The layout organizes offerings into clear sections—Sides, Drinks, and Single/Family Meals—with appetizing food illustrations that enhance visual appeal. Bold typography and clean pricing structure ensure easy navigation, while special emphasis on signature items like "Mel Val's World Famous Aji Chilli" reinforces brand uniqueness.


The design effectively positions Peru Chicken as both a family restaurant and catering service, with authentic Peruvian offerings at accessible price points that appeal to diverse customer segments while maintaining strong brand consistency throughout.

The American Hotdog Company Menu

This menu design celebrates American street food culture through bold patriotic imagery featuring the Statue of Liberty and flag elements. The dynamic layout uses vibrant yellow typography against a dark background, while city-inspired names like "The Chicago Dog" and "The New Yorker" reinforce authentic American dining experiences.


The design strategically organizes specialty hotdogs with detailed descriptions and appetizing photography, using directional arrows and varied text sizes to guide customers through offerings ranging from $6-12. Creative highlights like "Cheese No Charge" and the "Sides" section with jalapeño poppers and loaded gems add visual interest.


The patriotic theme combined with mouth-watering visuals creates an engaging brand experience that positions The American Hotdog Company as an authentic purveyor of classic American comfort food with energetic, casual street food atmosphere.

Ariston-Ravinia Email Signature

This professional email signature features a dual-brand approach showcasing both Ariston and Ravinia logos with their shared tagline "Quality Products - Right Price." The modern navy and white color scheme creates a sophisticated corporate identity suitable for the procurement and sales industry.


The layout balances branding elements on the left with comprehensive contact information on the right, including phone, email, website, and social media icons. Clean typography and geometric design elements ensure excellent readability across email platforms while maintaining visual professionalism.


The signature effectively positions Aris Kalantzopoulos as a reliable contact for both brands, suggesting comprehensive market coverage while facilitating easy business communication through streamlined contact layout and accessible design.
